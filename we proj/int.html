How is Mr. Robot?

American psychological television series, created by Sam Esmail. The pilot episode "Mr. Robot" was shown at the SXSW festival and then released on YouTube for free. The audience liked the first episode so much that the decision on the second season was made even before the first [6]. Season 2 start date - 13 July 2016.

About series

In the center of the plot of the series is the young hacker Elliot Alderson. He lives in New York. Suffers from clinical depression and paranoia.

The show's author Sam Esmail has been developing it for 15 years. He wanted to show the real life of hackers.

The series has 4 seasons. and each season make one of the best TV series in this genre as a masterpiece
